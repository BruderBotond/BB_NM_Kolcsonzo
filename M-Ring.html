M-Ring

The Ultimate Playground for Driving Enthusiasts

The M-Ring is a hidden gem among Hungary’s racetracks, offering a thrilling and versatile driving experience for all motorsport lovers. Located in Kunmadaras, this modern circuit is designed to cater to a wide range of driving activities, from high-speed laps to precision handling tests.

Spanning 2.53 km with a variety of corners and straights, the M-Ring is the ideal track for drivers looking to improve their skills or simply enjoy the thrill of driving in a controlled environment. Its unique layout allows for an engaging and dynamic experience, no matter your level of expertise.
