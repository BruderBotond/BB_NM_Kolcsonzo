Balaton Park Circuit

A New Jewel for Motorsport Enthusiasts

The Balaton Park Circuit is Hungary's newest state-of-the-art racetrack, located near the picturesque Lake Balaton. Designed to meet the highest international standards, this modern facility combines cutting-edge technology with a scenic setting, making it a standout destination for motorsport lovers.

With a total length of 4.115 km and 16 dynamic turns, the circuit offers an exciting and technical driving experience for racers of all levels. Its smooth asphalt and challenging layout make it ideal for pushing the limits of both the car and the driver, whether you're an amateur enthusiast or a seasoned professional.
