The Home of Speed and Excitement

Hungaroring is Hungary's most famous and internationally renowned racing circuit, located near Budapest in a picturesque setting. Built in 1986, it has hosted the Hungarian Grand Prix ever since, making it one of the longest-running events on the Formula 1 calendar. Known for its technical layout and thrilling corners, the track offers an exhilarating experience for both professional drivers and enthusiasts alike. With a total length of 4.381 km and 14 challenging turns, Hungaroring is perfect for testing your driving skills and enjoying high-performance vehicles to their fullest potential.
