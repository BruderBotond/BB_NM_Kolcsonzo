The Perfect Track for Adrenaline Seekers

Located in the heart of Hungary, the DRX Ring is a versatile racetrack designed for a wide range of motorsport activities. Known for its dynamic layout and excellent facilities, it’s a favorite destination for those who crave speed and precision driving.

With its 1.5 km circuit featuring technical corners, quick straights, and elevation changes, the DRX Ring offers a challenging yet rewarding driving experience. Whether you're looking to fine-tune your racing skills or simply enjoy the thrill of high-performance driving, this track is the ideal playground.
